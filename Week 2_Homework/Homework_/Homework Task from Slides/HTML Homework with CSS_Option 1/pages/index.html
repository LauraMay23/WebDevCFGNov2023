How to Make the Perfect Barista-Style Hot Chocolate

This blog will explain how to make the perfect hot chocolate, just in time for the cold winter season!

What You Will Need:

  • Hot chocolate powder
  • A coffee machine with a steaming wand or a milk frother
  • Your preferred choice of milk (Mine is full-fat blue milk but you can use any kind you like, such as semi-skimmed, oat, coconut etc!)
  • A steaming milk jug or your favourite mug

Preparation:

  1. First, combine 4 heaped tablespoons of hot chocolate powder and milk into a metal steaming jug (or mug)
  2. Second, using either the steaming wand or your milk frother, blend the two together until they reach a velvety consistency
  3. Third, if using a steaming jug, pour into your mug and then top with your favourite toppings, such as cream and marshmallows.
  4. Finally, serve and enjoy!
